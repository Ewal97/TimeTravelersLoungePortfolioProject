A Trip to Parliament: Recipe

A Trip to Parliament

  • 1 ml Orange Flower Water
  • 1 Large Egg white
  • 1/2 oz or 15ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • 1/2 oz or 15ml Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1 oz or 30 ml 1:1 Simple Syrup
  • 1.5 oz or 45 ml London Dry gin

Combine ingredients into shaker, Boston shaker preferred, and shake once with ice. Double strain by pouring through a Hawthorn Strainer into a fine mesh siv sitting on the other half of the shaker. Discard ice. Reseal the shaker and dry shake till foamy. Pour into awaiting rocks glass, and garnish with an expressed lemon zest.
