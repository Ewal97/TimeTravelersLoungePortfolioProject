Amongst the Clouds

  • 3 Dashes Lime Coriander Bitters
  • 1/2 oz or 15ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • 1 oz or 30 ml Coconut Cream
  • 1 oz or 30 ml 1:1 Simple Syrup
  • 1 oz or 30 ml soda/sparkling water
  • 1.5 oz or 45 ml Hibiscus Infused White Rum

Combine ingredients into shaker, Boston shaker preferred, and shake with ice. Strain into awaiting Collin's glass, top with soda water carefully to build foam, and garnish with an fan of lime slices and two straws.
