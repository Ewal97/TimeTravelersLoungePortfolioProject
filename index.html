Time Traveler's Lounge is a place beyond time. Come experience an ever changing trip through time. Expect the unexpected along side some tasty classics. The future meets the past, the furniture doesn't match, and the atmosphere is cozy.

Time Traveler's Lounge is a 21 and over EXCLUSIVE cocktail bar. No outside food or drink is permitted, unless specifically requested for special events. You may request reservations for special events by emailing us at the email provided on the bottom of the page or on the contact us page.
