Menu:

Amongst the Clouds:

A strange and interesting drink, with a wonderful creaminess from coconut cream, bright citrus flavors, and sparkling water.

A Trip to Parliament:

A bright and refreshing gin sour, made with fresh citrus and orange flower water.

Classic Daiquiri:

Hard to go wrong with the refreshing simplicity of Rum, Fresh lime, and some simple syrup. A favorite classic that is always pleasant.

Deep Gamma:

A strange and strong cocktail, built around the intense flavors of Absinthe and Creme de Violette.

Gin Daisy:

The daisy is the original cocktail that became the Margarita! So this is baically just a bright and citrusy drink made with Gin, Citrus Liqueur, and lime!

Margarita:

Another classic. Tequila, citrus liqueur, and lime. Served up in a salted glass. Fun fact: Margarita is simply Daisy in spanish, since a Margarita is just a Tequila Daisy!

Ramos Gin Fizz:

Another classic, and a wonderful workout for our bartenders. The Ramos gin fizz is bright, a little strange, and quite refreshing. Made with Gin, egg white, cream, lime, and orange flower water, topped with soda water.

Whiskey Sour:

To finish out the classics, the Whiskey sour. Sweet and sour, with the oak-y pleasantries of whiskey. Your choice of our Whiskey selection, mixed with lemon, simple, and an egg white for a fluffy treat!
